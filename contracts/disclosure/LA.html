SPECIAL LOUISIANA REQUIREMENTS AND DISCLOSURES

The following State Specific Requirements apply if your CONTRACT was purchased in LOUISIANA and supersede any other provision herein of the contrary

The CANCELLATION PROCEDURES section is amended as follows: In the event a request from the PURCHASER/LENDER/PROVIDER is made within sixty (60) days of purchase, a flat cancellation will be allowed. If the PURCHASER/LENDER/PROVIDER requests a cancellation after sixty (60) days, the ADMINISTRATOR agrees to calculate and make available a pro-rata refund percentage figure based on the time or mileage, whichever refund is less. All other language reflected in this section remains unchanged.
